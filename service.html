Services That We Offer For You

Free Transportation

Transporting large purchases can be expensive and tiresome. To solve this with ease of commute, doorstep delivery is a free service we at Balaji Nursery are offering in certain areas. We provide delivery services however, widespread in all over India. Our motto is to ease the farming needs for farmers, and doorstep delivery is a tool of convenience for our entire clientele.

Free Consultation

Our free consultation service solves any problems that farmers face regarding re-farming needs. As we specialize in Agronomy consultation, we can guide our customers through the land restoration process, methods to maximize the food and fiber crop yields, improve the quality and fertility of the soil and other concerns without charging anything. With best practices for improving crop yields, we aim to simplify the farming life-cycle and bring prosperity to our farming clientele.
